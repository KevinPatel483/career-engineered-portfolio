Professional Direction

I want to become an AI Product Engineer.

This role connects software development, product thinking, user needs, automation, and AI tools. I chose this direction because I want to build practical systems that help people solve real problems.

What this role does

Applied problem-solving with AI and software

An AI Product Engineer works on products that use AI in a useful way. The work can include planning features, writing code, testing ideas, connecting tools, improving workflows, and making sure the product makes sense for real users.

Why it fits me
